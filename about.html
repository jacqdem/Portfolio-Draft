About the Artist

coder · researcher · strategist · designer


With an academic and professional background in social research, advocacy and education, I know first hand how human-centered design can effect and shape our collective experiences living, learning and connecting. Teaching myself coding and visual design fundamentals has been a natural evolution that continues to challenge me creatively and mentally. Combining these disciplines has given me the opportunity to be directly involved in creating dynamic, accessible and human-centered products that help real people.



creative · empathetic · thoughtful · curious


Creativity and curiousity drive my designs. My goal is always to create tangible results that help real people and contribute to positive change. I'm passionate about projects that promote diversity, inclusivity, accessibility, equity, sustainability and community wellbeing.
