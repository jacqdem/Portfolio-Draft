Glass Half Full

Ideation, Research, Design

For travelling feminists, wine lovers and amateur oenophiles alike, this web app will help you find woman owned and run vineyards, wineries, and wine shops across Italy.

It’s ‘wine o’clock’ somewhere!

Learn more about and support the women that make your favorite Italian wines. You can find contact information for each place, favorite the places you want to visit and leave reviews once you’ve visited. Let’s increase visibility and representation together!
