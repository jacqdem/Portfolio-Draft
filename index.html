Glass Half Full

Ideation, Research, Design

For travelling feminists, wine lovers and amateur oenophiles alike, this web app will help you find woman owned and run vineyards, wineries, and wine shops across Italy.

It’s ‘wine o’clock’ somewhere!

Learn more about and support the women that make your favorite Italian wines. You can find contact information for each place, favorite the places you want to visit and leave reviews once you’ve visited. Let’s increase visibility and representation together!

About the Artist

creative, empathetic, thoughtful, curious

coding, research, strategy, design

I'm an Italo-American currently based in Italy. I studied Sociology at uni where I grew my roots as a frenzied researcher and an advocate for social equity and justice. Upon graduation I became an educator which allowed me to flex my empathetic and creative muscles. Looking for a challenge I taught myself how to code. Coding and front-end web development, along with my desire to make everything look pretty, introduced me to UI/UX Design.

I utilize my passion for connecting with people along with research, information architecture and visual design skills to create recognizable branding and design dynamic, accessible, consumer-centered products.

Creativity and curiosity drive my designs, while my problem solving skills lend themselves to a solution oriented approach. The goal is always to create tangible results that contribute to positive change.

I'm passionate about projects that promote diversity, inclusivity, accessibility, equity, sustainability and community wellbeing.
